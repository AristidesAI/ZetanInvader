# Privacy Policy for Zetan Invader & Vortex Watch
Last updated: 06/01/2026
## Data Collection
Zetan Invader nor VortexWatch does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
## What We Don't Collect
- No personal information
- No location data
- No usage analytics
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party tracking
## Local Data
Game scores and settings are stored locally on your Apple Watch only and are never transmitted.
## Children's Privacy
Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 9.
