:: LORE DATABASE ::

ARCHIVE REFERENCE: Holotape "Zeta Invaders" [Source: Nick Valentine's Desk / Diamond City]

Recovered from the archives of the Commonwealth, the Zetan Invaders program was originally a static defense simulation found on RobCo Fun magazines. The goal was simple: destroy the alien fleet before they made planetfall.

UPDATE: This software has been recompiled for modern wrist-mounted hardware (watchOS). The simulation has evolved. You are no longer manning a turret; you are piloting an interceptor. The Zetan threat is now a survival endurance test through a digital asteroid field.

:: FLIGHT MANUAL ::

Pilot your vessel through the void while dodging debris and returning fire. A nostalgic green-on-black dot matrix display brings the retro-future to your wrist.

  • DIGITAL CROWN: Rotate for precision steering [Left/Right].
  • TAP SCREEN: Engage plasma cannons.
  • HAPTICS: Force feedback enabled for impacts and weapons fire.

WARNING: One hit from an asteroid or enemy fighter results in immediate hull failure (Game Over).

:: PRIVACY PROTOCOL ::

STATUS: SECURE

Zetan Invader does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no tracking beacons, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party uplinks.

All flight logs (High Scores) are stored locally on your device.
